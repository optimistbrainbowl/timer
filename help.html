Optimist Brain Bowl Timer

Open timer

This progressive web app provides a basic timing utility for Optimist Brain Bowl competitions.

  • Installation instructions:
    • Android: Open in your favorite web browser (known to work on Chrome and Firefox). Tap the three dots in the upper right of the browser and select "Add to Home screen".
    • iOS: Open in Safari. In the browser controls, tap the icon that's a square with an arrow coming up out of it. Scroll over and select "Add to Home Screen".
  • Usage instructions:
    • For settings, hit the button in the upper left corner
    • For a 5 second timer (second team when question was read fully), hit the button across the bottom of the screen
    • For a 15 second timer (first team or second team when question was not read fully), hit anywhere else on the screen
  • Features:
    • Voice or beep for time up (voice on by default; beep recommended for non-Apple devices)
    • Speech speed and voice settings (depends on the available voices on your device)
    • Persistent setting storage - your settings last until you clear the cache
    • Optional dark mode
    • Prebuilt functionality to allow warning sounds and changing timing intervals (not enabled on all distributions)
